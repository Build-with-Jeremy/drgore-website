# why-lousy-relationships

Why do we end up with our partners?   Why am I not finding a suitable partner?  It is easy to look outside of ourselves and say stuff like “all men fear commitment” or “all women are looking for perfection that doesn’t exist.”  However, who we end up with says a lot about who we are. This video gives some insight into who we date and who ends up moving in with us.
