# books-i-am-not-writing

2.	How to Raise a Happy Child by Being Their Best Friend

3.	Thinking Your Way to a Good Life

4.	Stopping Substance Abuse by Exerting Lots of Willpower

5.	Following the Gut Feelings of Others

6.	Small Talk and Ass Kissing: How to Succeed in a Corporate Environment
